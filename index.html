Our Story

From Sacred
Lotus Ponds
To Your Hands

Swaraj Makhana was born from a simple belief — India's most ancient superfood deserves to reach every corner of the world. We partner directly with farmers in Bihar's Mithila region, where makhana (fox nuts) have been harvested for over 3,000 years.

Every pouch carries the story of lotus ponds, skilled hands, and a tradition that's survived millennia. We keep that tradition alive — with zero additives, zero compromise.
